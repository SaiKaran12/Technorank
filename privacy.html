PRIVACY POLICY

At TechnoRank Solutions, we are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring that your privacy is respected. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.


1. What Personal Data Do We Collect?

We collect personal data that you provide to us, such as:
- Your name and contact information (e.g., email address, phone number)
- Your job title and company name (if applicable)
- Any other information you provide to us through our website, email, or other means


2. How Do We Use Your Personal Data?

We use your personal data for the following purposes:
- To provide you with information about our products and services
- To respond to your inquiries and requests
- To improve our website and services
- To comply with applicable laws and regulations


3. How Do We Protect Your Personal Data?

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:
- Encryption of sensitive data
- Secure socket layer (SSL) technology
- Firewalls and intrusion detection systems
- Access controls and authentication procedures


4. Who Do We Share Your Personal Data With?

We may share your personal data with:
- Our affiliates and subsidiaries
- Our service providers and contractors
- Law enforcement agencies or regulatory authorities (if required by law)


5. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Request access to your personal data
- Request correction or deletion of your personal data
- Object to the processing of your personal data
- Request restriction of processing of your personal data

We reserve the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. Your continued use of our website or services shall be deemed acceptance of any changes to this Privacy Policy.


7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
